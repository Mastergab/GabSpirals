Welcome to the Little Burgundy Employment Center practice Exercice to learn how to fill Job Application Forms. We will ask you to fill out the following form as best you can, and give you feedback on how you did when you press the SUBMIT button at the bottom of the page. Start when you are ready. TIP: If you want to save yourself some typing time, and your CV is already done, you can open your CV in Word while keeping this window open, and copy-paste the information from your CV into this form. Remember, copying can be done with the keyboard shortcut CTRL-C, and pasting can be done with CTRL-V.
